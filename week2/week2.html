AMRITA VISHWA VIDYAPEETAM

      Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, born as Sudhamani Idamannel on September 27, 1953,is lovingly known as Amma,
       meaning “Mother,” and also called the “hugging saint.” She is a world-famous Indian spiritual leader
        and humanitarian from Kerala.She is internationally known for embracing millions of people as a 
         deep expression of compassion, spreading unconditional love to relieve physical and emotional pain.
