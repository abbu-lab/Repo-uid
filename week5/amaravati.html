Amaravati Campus

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Amaravati Campus is situated in Andhra Pradesh, close to the banks of the Krishna River. The campus offers well-equipped modern classrooms, advanced research laboratories, and comfortable hostel facilities to support students’ academic and personal growth.

Popular Courses: CSE, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science Facilities: Library, smart classrooms, hostel accommodation Focus: Encouraging innovation and promoting strong research culture
